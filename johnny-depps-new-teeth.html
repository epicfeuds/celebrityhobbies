Biography of Johnny Depp

Johnny Depp was born on June 9, 1963, in Owensboro, Kentucky. He is an American actor, producer, and musician, renowned for his versatile roles in films ranging from blockbusters to independent films. Depp gained fame in the early 1990s with his role in the television series "21 Jump Street" and later achieved international stardom through his portrayal of Captain Jack Sparrow in the "Pirates of the Caribbean" franchise.
