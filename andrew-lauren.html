Biography of Andrew Lauren

Andrew Lauren, born on January 26, 1974, is the son of Ralph Lauren, an iconic figure in the fashion industry, and Ricky Lauren. Growing up in a household steeped in creativity, Andrew was exposed to the arts from a young age. He attended the Duke University, where he majored in sociology and gained a broad understanding of cultural dynamics.
