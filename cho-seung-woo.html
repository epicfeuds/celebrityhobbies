Cho Seung-woo was born in South Korea and showed an early interest in music and performance. Growing up, he participated in various musical activities, honing his skills and preparing himself for a career in the entertainment industry. His dedication and passion for music were evident from a young age, paving the way for his future in K-Pop.

Seung-woo attended a performing arts school, where he refined his vocal abilities and gained experience in stage performances. His hard work paid off when he auditioned for a major entertainment company, leading to his training as a trainee artist.

Musical Career

Cho Seung-woo's musical career began when he joined a well-known entertainment company as a trainee. After years of rigorous training, he was finally ready to debut and showcase his talent to the world.

Debut with X1

In 2019, Cho Seung-woo participated in the reality survival show "Produce X 101," where he showcased his exceptional vocal and performance skills. His impressive journey on the show culminated in his debut as a member of X1, a project boy group formed through the series.

X1 quickly gained popularity, and their debut album was met with commercial success. The group was known for their powerful performances and catchy songs, and Seung-woo's contributions as a lead vocalist were vital to their acclaim.

Solo Activities

After X1 disbanded in early 2020, Cho Seung-woo embarked on a solo career. His solo debut was marked by the release of his first mini-album, which showcased his versatility as an artist. The album received positive reviews and solidified his status as a solo artist in the K-Pop scene.

Seung-woo continued to release music that resonated with fans, exploring various genres and collaborating with other artists. His ability to connect with his audience through heartfelt lyrics and captivating performances has set him apart in the competitive industry.
