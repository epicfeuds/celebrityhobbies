Biography of Do Kyung-Soo

Do Kyung-Soo, commonly known as D.O., was born on January 12, 1993, in Goyang, South Korea. He is the youngest member of the popular boy band EXO, formed by SM Entertainment in 2012. D.O. exhibited a passion for music from a young age, participating in various singing competitions and eventually earning a spot in EXO due to his exceptional vocal abilities.
