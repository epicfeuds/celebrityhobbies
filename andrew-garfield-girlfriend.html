Biography of Andrew Garfield

Andrew Garfield was born on August 20, 1983, in Los Angeles, California. He is a British-American actor known for his versatile roles in both film and theater. Garfield gained significant recognition after starring in "The Amazing Spider-Man" series, where he played the iconic superhero. His other notable works include "The Social Network," "Hacksaw Ridge," and "Tick, Tick... Boom!" His performances have garnered numerous awards and nominations, establishing him as one of the leading actors of his generation.
