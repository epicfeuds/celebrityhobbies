The History of Sanrio

Founded in 1960 by Shintaro Tsuji, Sanrio started as a company that sold small gifts and greeting cards. The first character, Kitty White, better known as Hello Kitty, was introduced in 1974 and quickly became a global icon. Sanrio's philosophy revolves around the notion of spreading happiness through cute characters, which has driven the company to create a diverse range of personalities over the decades.
