Biography of Jeffrey Dahmer

Jeffrey Lionel Dahmer, also known as the Milwaukee Cannibal, was born on May 21, 1960, in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. He was an American serial killer and sex offender who committed the murder and dismemberment of 17 men and boys between 1978 and 1991. Dahmer's early life was marked by a troubled childhood, characterized by his parents' tumultuous marriage and his own struggles with mental health.
