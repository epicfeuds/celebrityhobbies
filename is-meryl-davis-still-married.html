Biography of Meryl Davis

Meryl Elizabeth Davis was born on January 1, 1987, in Royal Oak, Michigan. She began skating at a young age and quickly showed exceptional talent. Over the years, she trained rigorously, dedicating herself to the sport of figure skating. Her partnership with Charlie White began in 1997, and together they became one of the most successful ice dancing teams in history.
