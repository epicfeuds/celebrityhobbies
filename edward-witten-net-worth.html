Early Life and Education

Edward Witten grew up in a family that valued education and intellectual discourse. He attended the Baltimore City College High School, where he excelled in science and mathematics. Witten's academic journey continued at Princeton University, where he completed his Ph.D. in 1976 under the supervision of David Gross, another esteemed physicist. His early exposure to complex scientific concepts laid the groundwork for his future contributions to the field.
