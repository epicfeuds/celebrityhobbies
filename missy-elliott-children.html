Biography of Missy Elliott

Missy Elliott, born Melissa Arnette Elliott on July 1, 1971, in Portsmouth, Virginia, is celebrated for her innovative music and unique style. She rose to fame in the late 1990s with hit albums like "Supa Dupa Fly" and has since become a cultural icon. Beyond her musical achievements, her personal life, particularly her role as a mother, is a significant part of her identity.
