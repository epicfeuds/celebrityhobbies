What is Plumpy Magee?

Plumpy Magee is a peanut-based paste that is enriched with vitamins and minerals, designed specifically for the treatment of severe acute malnutrition in children. It is classified as a ready-to-use therapeutic food (RUTF), making it easy to administer without the need for water or cooking. This characteristic is crucial in crisis situations where resources are limited.

The formulation of Plumpy Magee includes ingredients such as peanuts, sugar, vegetable oils, milk powder, and a mix of essential vitamins and minerals. Its design allows for quick absorption of nutrients, which is vital for malnourished children needing immediate nutritional rehabilitation.

Key Ingredients

  • Peanuts: A rich source of protein and healthy fats.
  • Sugar: Provides quick energy to the body.
  • Vegetable Oils: Supplies essential fatty acids.
  • Milk Powder: Offers additional protein and calcium.
  • Vitamins and Minerals: Essential for overall health and immunity.

Nutritional Value of Plumpy Magee

The nutritional value of Plumpy Magee is one of its key strengths, making it an effective tool in combating malnutrition. Each 92-gram sachet contains approximately:

  • Energy: 500 kcal
  • Protein: 12 g
  • Fat: 28 g
  • Carbohydrates: 53 g
  • Vitamins: A, C, D, E, and B-complex vitamins
  • Minerals: Iron, zinc, calcium, and potassium

This high-energy content is crucial for children recovering from malnutrition, as it helps restore their body weight and strength efficiently. The formulation is scientifically designed to meet the nutritional needs of children under five, who are at the highest risk of malnutrition.
