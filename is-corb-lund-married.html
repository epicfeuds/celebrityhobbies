Biography of Corb Lund

Corb Lund was born on January 10, 1975, in Taber, Alberta, Canada. He grew up in a ranching family, which deeply influenced his music and writing. Lund’s upbringing in the Canadian prairies exposed him to the lifestyle and culture that he often references in his songs. He attended the University of Alberta, where he studied anthropology and earned a degree.
