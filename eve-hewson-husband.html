Biography of Eve Hewson

Eve Hewson was born on July 7, 1991, in Dublin, Ireland. She is the daughter of Bono, the lead singer of the iconic rock band U2, and Ali Hewson, a businesswoman and activist. Growing up in a creative environment greatly influenced her passion for acting and the arts.

Early Life and Education

Eve grew up in a family that encouraged creativity and self-expression. She attended the prestigious Dublin's French School before moving to the United States to pursue her acting career. Eve later studied drama at New York University, where she honed her skills and developed a passion for storytelling.
